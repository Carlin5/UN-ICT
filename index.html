About UN-ICT

The United Nations ICT Department is dedicated to leveraging technology for sustainable development and humanitarian assistance across Africa. We work to bridge the digital divide and empower communities through innovative technological solutions.

Our mission is to support UN agencies and member states in their digital transformation journey while ensuring equitable access to technology and information.

Our Services

Digital Infrastructure

Building and maintaining robust ICT infrastructure for UN operations across Africa.

Cybersecurity

Ensuring secure and protected digital environments for UN operations.

Capacity Building

Training and development programs for UN staff and partner organizations.

Digital Transformation

Supporting UN agencies in their digital transformation initiatives.
